Software Requirements Specification (SRS)
 Railway Network Scheduling System

1. Introduction
This system manages railway train scheduling, track allocation, and prevents scheduling conflicts.

2. Objectives
- Manage train schedules
- Allocate tracks to trains
- Prevent collisions and conflicts
- Ensure correct timing of trains

3. Functional Requirements
- System shall add train schedule
- System shall assign tracks to trains
- System shall update train status
- System shall detect scheduling conflicts

4. Non-Functional Requirements
- System shall ensure safety
- System shall maintain consistency
- System shall support verification
